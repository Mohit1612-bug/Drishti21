Kavya Sammelan


Kavya Sammelan is a much awaited talent-based entertainment event organized by team Drishti wherein students from our colleges participate and exhibit their talent.

This event includes singing, dancing, drama, poetry and much more. Eminent college clubs like FSDC , Avaroh and Gandharva participate and showcase their creativity. Even clubs like Sankalp participate enthusiastically in this event.

This year Kavya Sammelan witnessed its 1st online edition wherein participants had to record short video clips of their performances. These clips were then arranged and were presented on the official channel of Team Drishti on YouTube.

The response to this online event was overwhelming which motivated the team to come up with another edition of the same event named Kavya Sammelan 2.0.

Face Painting


Face painting competition was organised in college by team DRISHTI. Colourful faces which were beyond recognition were a delight for the students and teachers alike.

Various students participated in the competition and showed their creativity using the face as their canvas.They used fabric, poster, water and organic colors to paint the faces.

Taare Zameen Par


Taare zameen par is a drawing and sketching event organized by Team Drishti, which is very popular among children. Children from all over the city participate and sketch their ideas and creativity on papers.

Not only school going children but also college students and faculty participate enthusiastically in this event.The main idea behind organizing this event is to encourage children to be able to present their innocence and creativity in the form of art, it also instills an artistic value in them.

It helps the children find the inner Picasso in them.This event gives the college students a chance to awaken the child and artist in them and relive their childhood. Last year, this event was arranged on republic day and the response was truly exhilarating. This is a colorful event, full of joy and laughter, in the truest sense.
